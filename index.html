From Airport

There are several convenient ways to travel from Edinburgh Airport to the city center.

  • Airport Bus: The Airlink 100 bus runs regularly to Waverley Bridge in the city center.

  • Trams: Trams also run frequently from the airport into the city.

From the City Center to 103 Restalrig Road

Once you're in the city center, you can take the following Lothian buses to reach Restalrig Road.

  • Night buses: 25 or N25
